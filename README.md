### 📌 Project Executive Summary

This project implements a complete Supply Chain & Warehouse Analytics framework analyzing over **50,000 supply chain transactions** across products, customers, shipping operations, and regional performance. Through SQL-based data analysis, Power Query transformation, DAX KPI development, and interactive Power BI reporting, the solution delivers a consolidated business intelligence platform capable of supporting strategic and operational decision-making.

The final solution consists of **4 interconnected analytics dashboards** covering Executive Reporting, Product Analytics, Customer Analytics, and Supply Chain Performance Monitoring.

---

### 🛠️ Technical Workflow & Tools

* **Data Preparation & Transformation:** Imported and transformed 50K+ records using Power Query, performing data cleaning, validation, type conversions, and business rule implementation.
* **SQL Business Analysis:** Executed SQL queries for data exploration, aggregation, revenue analysis, customer analysis, shipping performance evaluation, and operational reporting.
* **Data Modeling:** Applied Star Schema principles and relationship modeling to create scalable and optimized reporting structures.
* **KPI Development:** Designed and implemented 20+ DAX measures including Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin, Customer Metrics, Delivery KPIs, and Supply Chain Performance indicators.
* **Dashboard Development:** Built interactive Power BI dashboards using KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, Treemaps, Maps, Trend Analysis, and Dynamic Filtering.

---

### 📈 Strategic Business Insights

* **Revenue Optimization:** Identified top-performing products, categories, and customer segments contributing the highest revenue and profitability.
* **Customer Intelligence:** Isolated high-value customer segments and analyzed geographic revenue distribution across multiple regions.
* **Supply Chain Monitoring:** Detected delivery bottlenecks and regions exhibiting elevated late-delivery risks.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Measured shipping performance through delivery time comparisons and logistics KPI monitoring.
* **Executive Reporting:** Developed a centralized business intelligence framework enabling data-driven strategic decision-making.
